Gage's Service

Here are some of the service events I have participated in. Click a box to see the details!

Guatemala Mission Trip

This was one of the best experiences I have ever had. I went with a church group to a small city in Guatemala called Santa Maria de Jesus. There with a team of 5 others, we constructed a new home for a family with hammers, nails, plywood, and corrugated iron, and provided them with food, clothing, household goods, and toys for the children. The city pictured in the background is the beautiful Guatemalan city of Antigua.

Peer Notetaker

I have signed up to be a peer notetaker for 5 of my classes taken at A&M. The idea of the program is to take electronic notes during class for other Aggies with disabilities so that everyone can have access to high-quality notes. I have loved participating in the program and will continue to do so when possible.

Big Event

The "Big Event" at Texas A&M is the largest student-run service project in the nation. Aggies from Texas A&M come together to help residents in the community with anything from yard work, to painting, to window washing. I participated in the big event 2021 with my brother and a good friend. We worked together to remove the weeds from the yard of a wonderful young couple.
