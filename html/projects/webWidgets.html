A Hobby of Mine:
Web widgets

By Gage Broberg / January 31, 2022


As I'm hoping you will have noticed by now, I am not entirely new to web development! My mother was an art major in college, and although I can't manage to draw a straight line on a page, I still enjoy UI design and what better way to dip your toe in to UI then some good old fashioned HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. In my free time I like to create little web widgets, and I'd like to think I've learned a lot along the way!
